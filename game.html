Play a Game

Duration: 15 minutes

Accomplish perfect pronunciation.
Play a fun, interactive game!

  • 1. Divide the class into 2 groups.
  • 2. Flip a coin to decide who goes first.
  • 3. The teams take turns after each correct answer.
  • 4. The team that makes a mistake loses the round.
  • 5. The winning team gets 3 points.
  • 6. Repeat 3 times.

Classtime

As an ESL teacher, your aim will be to improve your students grammer, vocabulary and pronouciation. To keep your class engaged, a combination of games, flashcards, songs and speaking/writing exercises should be used. In the sample lesson below, your students will learn the names of four farmyard animals

Flashcards

Get your students speaking! Teach your students the following words, by repeating the following animal names out loud.

Learn a Song

Duration: 20 - 30 minutes

Reinforce the new vocabularly by teaching a fun song. First, let your students watch the song, then teach them the words. When they have mastered the words, play the video and sing the song together.

Play a memory Game

Duration: 15 minutes

Reward your students, by playing a fun, interactive game!

  • 1. Divide the class into 2 groups.
  • 2. Flip a coin to decide who goes first.
  • 3. Let the teams take turns after each correct answer.
  • 4. The team that makes an incorrect choice loses the round.
  • 5. The winning team gets 3 points.
  • 6. Repeat 3 times.

Learn a Song

Duration: 20 - 30 minutes

Reinforce the new vocabularly by teaching a fun song. First, let your students watch the song, then teach them the words. When they have mastered the words, play the video and sing the song together.

Play a memory Game

Duration: 15 minutes

Reward your students, by playing a fun, interactive game. Split the class into 2 teams, let each team take turns in every round. The team with the highest score wins.

1. Divide your class into two groups
2. Flip a coin to choose who goes First
3. Let the teams take turns after each correct answer
4. The team who make an incorrect choice loses and the points are given to the other team.
5. repeat 3 times.
